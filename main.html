About Noiré Luxe Hotel

Where luxury meets heritage in the heart of Kuching

Where Luxury Meets Heritage

Located in the heart of Kuching, THE NOIRÉ LUXE HOTEL offers an unparalleled luxury experience across 20 levels of exquisite accommodation. Our exclusive floors 15-20 provide breathtaking views of the city and Sarawak River.

Established in 2018, we've redefined hospitality in Malaysia by combining modern luxury with traditional Sarawakian warmth. Our commitment to excellence has earned us the "Luxury Hotel of the Year" award three years in a row.
